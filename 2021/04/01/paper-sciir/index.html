Paper-SciIR

Aminer[2008.kdd]

  • http://www.aminer.cn

Motivation(information cannot be modeled interact)

dblp , citeseer ， Google scholar

  • lacks semantic-based information
  • lack a unified approach to efficiently model the academic network
  • Different information modeled individually , and the interaction and dependencies cannot be captured !

Goals [抽取，整合，服务]

  • Extracting researcher profiles automatically from the web,
  • Intergrating the publications into the networks
  • Modeling entire academic network
  • 不仅仅搜索标题，作者，期刊，而且每一个学者的语言信息关系网的都能够建立

Related subtasks

  • topic modeling(LDA) ; Personal Profile Extraction ; Name Disambiguation(normalization)

five main components

  • person profile extraction ：
    • position ， affiliation ， email ， address , phone , fax ,phd
    • Crf tagging to get three feature ： content features， pattern features ， term feature.
  • name disambiguation: a probabilistic framework
  • Topic modeling (author-conference-topic) : documents + author information

Other Application

  • Expert finding ; Association search (find and rank); author interest finding and academic suggestions

Sci Document related Task

ArnetMiner : Extraction and Mining of Academic Socical NetWorks

Task1: Text Generation

Problem

  • Subtask: related work generation , multi-document summarization

  • meaning: reduce authot’s time and effort ,find the specific contribution of individual papers and arrange them in several sentences.

Method:

  • reference file:only abstract , introduce, related work ,conclusion sections ,target file :have already written abstract and introduction. [books and thesis was dropped!]
  • citation information is helpful for scientific summarization
  • Step1 : summarize the referenced paper , step2: compare with the target paper
  • Tool to parser PDF file: extract texts py PDFlib and pare sections with ParsCit

Conclustion

  • first exploits a probability latent semantic analysis [plsa] to topic-biased parts , and the applies regression models to learn the importance socres of the sentences. Finally , an optimization framework is proposed to generate the related work section.

Metric : rouge scores
